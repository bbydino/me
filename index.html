The origins of bbydino

My nickname is bbydino , pronounced as "baby dino". When I was 13, some of my friends told me that I looked like mixture of a duck and a dinosaur, which I thought was pretty funny. They called me 'duckosaurus', but I was like, "yo that doesn't sound as swaggy." Since people also said that I had a baby face (still do), they called me 'baby dinosaur'. But 'baby dinosaur' still didn't feel cool enough, so voila --the birth of bbydino .
